## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at **[KAIST](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/en/)**, advised by Prof. **[Kuk-Jin Yoon](http://vi.kaist.ac.kr/)** at the Visual Intelligence Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. (2026) and B.S. (2020) in Mechanical Engineering (minor in Electrical Engineering) from KAIST, with an exchange semester at the **Technical University of Denmark (DTU)** in 2019.

My research centers on **Computer Vision and AI**, with a focus on **generative models**, **reinforcement learning for vision**, **3D / novel-view synthesis**, and **computational photography**.

Feel free to reach me at **dudgh1732 (at) kaist.ac.kr**.

## Research Interests

- **Generative Models** — diffusion-based image/video/3D generation and editing
- **Reinforcement Learning for Vision** — RL-driven refinement of generative and perception models
- **3D Vision** — Gaussian Splatting, neural rendering, novel-view synthesis, implicit surface reconstruction
- **Computational Photography** — super-resolution, image restoration, adverse-weather removal, image stitching
